# Policy Resolution Template

## Resolution Title

Short name for the policy proposal.

## Sponsor

List the sponsoring riding association, commission, caucus, or member group.

## Resolution Type

- Policy Resolution
- Platform Directive
- Discussion Paper
- Emergency Resolution

## Problem Statement

State the issue the Party should address.

## Proposed Resolution

Write the policy direction in clear, affirmative language.

## Federal Jurisdiction

Explain how the matter connects to federal authority, spending, regulation, or
intergovernmental action.

## Evidence and Consultation

List research, lived experience, stakeholder consultation, or comparative models
that support the resolution.

## Fiscal or Administrative Considerations

Note major cost, implementation, or legal issues.

## Requested Convention Action

State whether the sponsor wants immediate adoption, referral for study, or
emergency debate.
