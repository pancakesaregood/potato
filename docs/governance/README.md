## Hierarchy and Authority

1. The constitution is the supreme internal authority of the party.
2. Bylaws govern recurring institutional functions under the constitution.
3. Process documents explain how text is drafted, reviewed, versioned, and
   recorded in Git.
4. Templates standardize submissions and do not create powers on their own.

## Ratification Map

| Document Type | Typical Ratifier | Threshold |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Constitution | National Convention delegates | Two thirds |
| Bylaws | National Convention delegates | Sixty percent |
| Provisional bylaw orders | National Council | Two thirds |
| Policy resolutions | Members or delegates under the policy bylaw | Simple majority unless otherwise stated |
| Editorial clarifications | Maintainers | No separate vote if non-substantive |

## Collaboration Rule

Merged text records the current state of drafting, but only the authorized
ratifying body can place a governance rule into force.
