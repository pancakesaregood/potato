# Article 04: Justice and Prison Reform

## Summary

This article frames justice policy around safety, rehabilitation, proportionality, and independent oversight.

## Principles

- Justice systems should reduce harm and protect due process.
- Prison should not be the default response to poverty, illness, or addiction.
- Oversight and transparency are necessary for public trust.

## Policy Mechanisms

- Expand diversion, restorative justice, and treatment-based sentencing options.
- Set national standards for prison health care, education, and reintegration planning.
- Publish comparable data on remand, incarceration conditions, and recidivism.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Recidivism rates fall for participating populations.
- More cases are resolved through diversion where public safety permits.
- Independent oversight reports are released on a predictable schedule.
