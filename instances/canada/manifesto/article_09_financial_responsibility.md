# Article 09: Financial Responsibility

## Summary

This article sets a disciplined fiscal approach that balances investment, resilience, and public transparency.

## Principles

- Budgets should be understandable, costed, and comparable over time.
- Borrowing should support durable public value, not avoidable waste.
- Program spending should face periodic review against outcomes.

## Policy Mechanisms

- Publish multi-year fiscal plans with baseline and stress-case scenarios.
- Require outcome reviews for major spending envelopes and tax expenditures.
- Maintain reserve and contingency rules for major economic shocks.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Budget reporting arrives on schedule and in plain language.
- Program reviews identify savings, failures, and successful scaling opportunities.
- Fiscal resilience metrics improve against published benchmarks.
