# Article 14: Ten Year Vision

## Summary

This article establishes a ten-year planning horizon so the party can link immediate policy choices to long-term goals.

## Principles

- Long-range planning should survive beyond one election cycle.
- Milestones should be public, measurable, and open to revision.
- Scenario planning should inform both optimism and risk management.

## Policy Mechanisms

- Publish a ten-year national roadmap with milestone reviews every two years.
- Tie manifesto updates to a standing dashboard of long-term indicators.
- Run scenario reviews for economic, climate, health, and democratic risks.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Milestone reports are published on the expected schedule.
- Long-term indicators are updated and linked to current policy choices.
- Revision history shows why strategic targets changed over time.
