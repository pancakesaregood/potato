# Article 03: Equality and Rights

## Summary

This article affirms that public policy should protect equal dignity, civil rights, and fair treatment under the law.

## Principles

- Public institutions must serve people without discrimination.
- Rights review should be built into policy design from the start.
- Accessibility and due process are part of equality, not optional extras.

## Policy Mechanisms

- Require rights-impact reviews for major federal programs and legislation.
- Expand accessibility standards in federal services, digital platforms, and public procurement.
- Strengthen complaint, remedy, and oversight pathways for rights violations.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Rights-impact reviews are published for major policy packages.
- Service barriers for disabled and marginalized residents decline over time.
- Complaint resolution data shows faster and fairer outcomes.
