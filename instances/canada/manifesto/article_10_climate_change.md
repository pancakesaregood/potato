# Article 10: Climate Change

## Summary

This article covers climate mitigation and adaptation through long-range planning, infrastructure renewal, and public accountability.

## Principles

- Climate policy should combine urgency with practical transition planning.
- Adaptation is as important as mitigation for community resilience.
- Major emitters should face clear targets and transparent reporting.

## Policy Mechanisms

- Adopt binding emissions pathways and sector plans with annual disclosure.
- Scale building retrofits, clean power integration, and resilience infrastructure.
- Require federal adaptation plans for wildfire, flood, heat, and coastal risk.

## Measurable Outcomes

- National emissions trend downward against published pathways.
- More homes and public buildings complete resilience and efficiency upgrades.
- Adaptation progress is measurable in high-risk communities.
