# Article 06: Environmental Responsibility

## Summary

This article establishes a broad stewardship framework covering land, water, waste, biodiversity, and public accountability.

## Principles

- Environmental responsibility includes restoration as well as protection.
- Polluters should bear real cleanup and compliance costs.
- Public reporting should make ecological performance easy to track.

## Policy Mechanisms

- Expand federal restoration funds tied to transparent local outcomes.
- Use procurement, regulation, and grants to support circular economy practices.
- Publish annual environmental accounts covering water, waste, habitat, and remediation.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Waste diversion and remediation metrics improve over time.
- Federal projects report ecological impacts using a common framework.
- Habitat restoration targets are measured and publicly updated.
