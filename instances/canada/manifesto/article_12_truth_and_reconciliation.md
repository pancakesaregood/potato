# Article 12: Truth and Reconciliation

## Summary

This article commits the PPPC to reconciliation that is led by action, partnership, and measurable accountability.

## Principles

- Reconciliation must respect Indigenous rights, jurisdiction, and knowledge.
- Federal institutions should be accountable for measurable follow-through.
- Policy design should support long-term relationships, not symbolic one-time gestures.

## Policy Mechanisms

- Track and publish progress on federal commitments linked to reconciliation.
- Support Indigenous-led service delivery, procurement, and data governance.
- Build co-development requirements into major federal decisions affecting Indigenous communities.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Progress reports show movement on published reconciliation commitments.
- Indigenous-led program delivery and procurement participation increase.
- Policy processes include earlier and more meaningful Indigenous involvement.
