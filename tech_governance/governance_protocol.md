# Governance Protocol

## Summary

This addendum describes the operational protocol for moving ideas from draft policy to governed implementation.

## Design Principles

- Every governance state should be explicit.
- Proposal handling should be consistent across topics.
- Appeals and corrections must be possible after automation runs.

## Technical Mechanisms

- Define proposal intake, review, revision, vote, and archival stages.
- Attach version numbers and status labels to major governance text.
- Require links between policy articles, proposals, and ratifying bodies.

## Validation Targets

- Proposal states are visible and searchable.
- Merged text can be traced to its proposal history.
- Archived proposals remain available for review and learning.
