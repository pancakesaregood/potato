# Consensus Mechanism

## Summary

This addendum outlines how the PPPC can reach trustworthy digital consensus on proposals, amendments, and governance decisions.

## Design Principles

- Consensus must be auditable and understandable.
- Human override remains available for contested cases.
- The mechanism should resist manipulation without excluding ordinary users.

## Technical Mechanisms

- Use staged proposal states such as draft, review, vote, and ratified.
- Separate identity verification from ballot secrecy and result publication.
- Publish decision logs, quorum rules, and challenge windows for major votes.

## Validation Targets

- Decision states are consistently recorded.
- Close votes can be independently recounted or reviewed.
- Members can understand how a proposal moved to final status.
