# Harmonized Healthcare

## Summary

This addendum describes a digital governance layer for portable, comparable, and privacy-aware health administration.

## Design Principles

- Care should remain portable across regions where the law allows.
- Data sharing must be consent-aware and purpose-limited.
- Technical standards should reduce fragmentation in basic service delivery.

## Technical Mechanisms

- Define common data standards for referrals, claims, and care continuity.
- Support interoperable patient and provider identifiers with privacy safeguards.
- Track service delays, portability failures, and administrative friction.

## Validation Targets

- Patients experience fewer administrative breaks across jurisdictions.
- Health data exchanges follow documented standards and controls.
- Portability and wait-time metrics are reported consistently.
