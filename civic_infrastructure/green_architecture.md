# Green Architecture

## Summary

This addendum sets environmental standards for the digital and physical infrastructure associated with PPPC governance systems.

## Design Principles

- Infrastructure choices should account for energy, materials, and lifecycle impact.
- Resilience and sustainability should be designed together.
- Measurement should guide infrastructure improvement over time.

## Technical Mechanisms

- Prefer low-energy hosting, efficient software, and sustainable procurement practices.
- Track the footprint of major digital services and public-facing infrastructure.
- Use green building and low-carbon design criteria in future delivery projects.

## Validation Targets

- Infrastructure energy use is measured and reduced where practical.
- Hosting and procurement decisions include lifecycle criteria.
- Resilience and environmental metrics are published together for major systems.
