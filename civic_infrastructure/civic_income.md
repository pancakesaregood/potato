# Civic Income

## Summary

This addendum outlines the technical and administrative design space for civic income or guaranteed-income style pilots.

## Design Principles

- Eligibility rules must be clear and fair.
- Payment systems should be simple, low-friction, and auditable.
- Pilot design should capture both economic and social outcomes.

## Technical Mechanisms

- Model secure identity, eligibility, payment, and appeals workflows.
- Separate policy entitlement rules from payment execution systems.
- Publish metrics for uptake, errors, fraud controls, and participant outcomes.

## Validation Targets

- Pilots can report payment accuracy and timeliness.
- Appeals and corrections are handled through a tracked process.
- Outcome reporting shows whether the model improves stability for participants.
