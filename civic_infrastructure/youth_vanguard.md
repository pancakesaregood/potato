# Youth Vanguard

## Summary

This addendum describes a youth-facing participation layer for civic education, leadership development, and policy experimentation.

## Design Principles

- Youth participation should be meaningful, not symbolic.
- Digital tools should make it easier for young people to organize safely.
- Leadership pipelines should be open and measurable.

## Technical Mechanisms

- Create youth consultation channels, training paths, and proposal sponsorship tools.
- Support low-cost digital participation for youth commissions and local organizers.
- Track mentorship, leadership participation, and policy contributions by youth members.

## Validation Targets

- Youth participation rates increase over time.
- More youth-authored proposals move into formal review.
- Leadership development programs show measurable retention and progression.
