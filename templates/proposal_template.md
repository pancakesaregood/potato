# Proposal Template

## Proposal Title

State the short name of the proposal.

## Problem Statement

Describe the public problem or governance gap this proposal addresses.

## Policy Mechanism

Explain the rule, program, or implementation mechanism being proposed.

## Constitutional Review

Describe Charter, constitutional, jurisdictional, and due-process questions.

## Economic Impact

Explain expected costs, savings, incentives, and fiscal tradeoffs.

## Environmental Impact

Explain expected environmental effects, including risks and mitigations.

## Citizen Feedback

Summarize consultations, member input, or public feedback channels.

## Implementation Plan

List the delivery steps, dependencies, timelines, and accountability owners.
