# Article 08: Governance and Accountability

## Summary

This article sets expectations for transparent public administration, anti-corruption safeguards, and measurable service quality.

## Principles

- Public office is a trust, not a private asset.
- Rules should be legible to citizens and enforceable in practice.
- Accountability should combine disclosure, oversight, and consequence.

## Policy Mechanisms

- Expand open contracting, lobbying disclosure, and public reporting obligations.
- Strengthen whistleblower protections and independent ethics review.
- Publish service-level targets for major federal programs and digital systems.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Audit findings are resolved within published timelines.
- More procurement and lobbying data is released in reusable formats.
- Service performance dashboards remain current and easy to inspect.
